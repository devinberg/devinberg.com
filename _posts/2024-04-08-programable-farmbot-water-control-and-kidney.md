## Authors
Jessica Qualich and Devin R. Berg

## Venue
National Conference on Undergraduate Research, Pittsburgh, PA.

## Abstract
The controlled environment of a greenhouse was used to grow kidney beans and develop an automated experimental design that can be applied to any plant to track variables using a 3-axis farming robot, FarmBot.
